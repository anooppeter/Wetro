The National Shipping Company of Saudi Arabia (Bahri) announces the deposit of the fractions shares sale arising out of the capital increase into shareholders' portfolios

14 JUN 2022

ELEMENT LIST EXPLANATION
Action Type Increase
Introduction The National Shipping Company of Saudi Arabia (Bahri) announces the completion of the selling of the fractions of shares arising out of the company’s capital increase, which based on the approval of the Extraordinary General Assembly Meeting and includes the capital increase, held on Monday 16 May 2022 Corresponding to 1443-10-15.
Fractional Shares Selling End Date 2022-05-23 Corresponding to 1443-10-22
Number of Sold Shares 10237
Fractional Shares Sales Revenue 260,589.93
Average Selling Price per share 25.50
Date of Depositing Fractional Shares Sales Revenue in the Bank Accounts of Eligible Investors 2022-06-14 Corresponding to 1443-11-15
Name of /Method of Contacting the Entity in Charge of Distributing the Fractional Shares Sales Revenue The proceeds resulted from selling the shares fractions were distributed by Riyad Bank using the bank accounts linked with the investment portfolios of the eligible shareholders.
