Wetrocomposite is a leading FRP manufacturing company

We manufacture FRP using Hand Lay-up and filament winding processes. We design, manufacture, supply, and install items made of FRP (GRP), PVC-FRP, CPVC-FRP, PP-FRP, PPH-FRP, PVDF-FRP, EPOXY, PHENOLIC, and FURANE. We are looking ahead to transform UAE into a key strategic regional hub for FRP manufacturing.
