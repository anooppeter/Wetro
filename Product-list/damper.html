About Wetrocomposite Dampers

Confused in how to select?..First get a clear picture on what are they: A damper in an HVAC system, also known as Duct Damper or Volume Balancing Damper is a movable plate, situated in the ductwork that regulates the flow of air and redirects it to specific places of a house. The usage of dampers is typically seen in zoning or zone control systems. Air Dampers are an essential part of those industries where gases and liquids are moved through pipes and ducts to provide control over the flow of materials.
