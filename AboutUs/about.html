About Us

About Wetrocomposite Industries LLC

We are engaged in design, manufacture, supply, installation and site jointing of various items made of FRP (GRP), PVC-FRP, CPVC-FRP, PP-FRP, PPH-FRP, PVDF-FRP, EPOXY, PHENOLIC, FURANE etc., used for different applications such as Process Equipment’s, Reaction Vessels, Storage Tanks, Pollution Control Equipment’s (Scrubbers, Separators, Blowers, Dampers, Ducts & Components, Stacks etc.), Absorbers, Towers, Dryers, exhaust Systems, Pipes Fittings, etc.

"We deliver Impactful Solution each and every time"

We are a leading FRP manufacturing company, Our FRP products are manufactured mainly by Hand Lay-up and filament winding processes.

FRP (Fiber glass Reinforced Plastics, also known as GRP, or Glass Reinforced Plastics) is a modern composite material of construction for chemical plant equipment like Tanks & vessels, Ducts & Components, Pipes & Fittings, etc.. Chemical equipment that range in various sizes is fabricated using FRP as material of construction. FRP products are manufactured mainly by Hand Lay-up and filament winding processes.
