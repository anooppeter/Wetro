Wetro Products

About Wetrocomposite Products

FRP (Fiber glass Reinforced Plastics, also known as GRP, or Glass Reinforced Plastics) is a modern composite material of construction for chemical plant equipment like Tanks & vessels, Ducts & Components, Pipes & Fittings, etc.. Chemical equipment that range in various sizes is fabricated using FRP as material of construction.
